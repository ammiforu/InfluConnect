# Brand FAQ

## Getting Started

### How do I sign up as a brand?
Visit the signup page and select "Brand" as your role. Enter your email, password, first name, and last name. After signing up, complete your brand profile with company details.

### What information do I need for my brand profile?
Your brand profile should include:
- **Company Name**: Your official business or brand name.
- **Industry**: Your business industry (e.g., Fashion, Technology, Food & Beverage, Health & Wellness, Entertainment).
- **Company Size**: Number of employees or business scale.
- **Website**: Your company website URL.
- **Description**: A brief overview of your brand and what you offer.
- **Logo**: Your company logo or brand image.
- **Location**: Your business headquarters location.

### How do I update my brand profile?
Navigate to the Profile page from the dashboard sidebar and click "Edit Profile" to modify your company information.

## Finding Influencers

### How do I search for influencers?
Go to the "Discover" page from the sidebar. You can search and filter influencers by:
- **Niche/Category**: Fashion, Tech, Food, Fitness, Travel, Beauty, Gaming, Lifestyle, etc.
- **Follower Count Range**: Filter by minimum and maximum followers.
- **Engagement Rate**: Filter by minimum engagement rate.
- **Platform**: Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Twitter, etc.
- **Location**: Filter by country or city.

### How do I view an influencer's full profile?
Click on an influencer card in the Discover page to view their full profile, including bio, social media stats, engagement rates, and past collaborations.

## Campaigns

### How do I create a campaign?
Go to the "Campaigns" page and click "Create Campaign." Fill in:
- **Campaign Name**: A descriptive title for your campaign.
- **Description**: Detailed campaign brief and objectives.
- **Budget**: Total campaign budget or per-influencer compensation.
- **Start Date & End Date**: Campaign timeline.
- **Deliverables Required**: What content pieces you need (posts, videos, stories, etc.).
- **Platform Requirements**: Which social media platforms the content should be posted on.
- **Target Audience**: Description of your target demographic.

### What are the campaign statuses?
Campaigns go through these statuses:
- **Draft**: Campaign is being created and not yet published.
- **Active**: Campaign is live and accepting collaborations.
- **In Progress**: Influencers are working on deliverables.
- **Completed**: All deliverables have been submitted and approved.
- **Cancelled**: Campaign has been cancelled.

### How do I send collaboration requests?
From an influencer's profile, click "Send Collaboration Request." Select the campaign, specify the compensation, and add any special instructions. The influencer will receive a notification.

## Deliverables

### How do I review deliverables?
Go to the campaign page and click on the "Deliverables" tab. You can see all submitted deliverables from influencers. For each deliverable, you can:
- **Approve**: Mark the deliverable as accepted.
- **Request Revision**: Ask the influencer to make changes.
- **Reject**: Decline the deliverable with feedback.

### What file types are supported for deliverables?
Influencers can submit deliverables as links to published content or upload files. Common supported formats include images (JPEG, PNG), videos (MP4), and documents (PDF).

## Messaging

### How does the messaging system work?
InfluConnect has a built-in real-time messaging system. You can communicate with influencers you have sent requests to or are collaborating with.

### Can I message any influencer?
You can message influencers that you have an active collaboration or pending request with. For initial contact, send a collaboration request first.

## Billing & Payments

### How does payment work?
Currently, InfluConnect facilitates the collaboration workflow. Payment terms are agreed upon between brands and influencers. The platform tracks deliverable completion to help manage payment milestones.

### Is there a subscription fee?
Contact InfluConnect for current pricing plans. The platform offers different tiers based on the number of campaigns and collaborations.

## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and enter your email to receive a reset link.

### Is my company data secure?
Yes. InfluConnect uses enterprise-grade security including Row-Level Security, encrypted communications, and secure data storage. Your company information is only visible according to your privacy settings.
